RENEWABLE ENERGY PRODUCTION


Renewable energy production is generates energy that produces no greenhouse gas emissions from fossil fuels and reduces some types of air pollution. Over the last decade the exponemtial development of new technologies have made it more commercially viable to harness energy from solar, wind, and geothermal sources.


However, one of the biggest concerns in the field of renewable energy is power generation depending on natural resources that are uncontrollable by humans e.g wind and sunlight. Additionally, renewable energy requires the use of significant amounts of land.


The Machine Learning Models use data from the U.S. Energy Information Administration to forecast the difference between Total Renewable Energy Production and Total Energy Consumption for the United States of America.
